## NOAA/NCEI IBTrACS

This project uses the International Best Track Archive for Climate Stewardship (IBTrACS), Version 4r01, North Atlantic subset, maintained by the NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information. The raw dataset is not redistributed in this repository. Users must obtain it from the [official NOAA/NCEI access page](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/international-best-track-archive) and follow the source provider's applicable terms, attribution guidance, and usage conditions.

Complete provenance, access instructions, checksums, and recommended citations are provided in [DATA_SOURCES.md](DATA_SOURCES.md).
