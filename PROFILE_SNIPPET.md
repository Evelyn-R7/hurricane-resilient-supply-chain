### Hurricane-Resilient Supply Chain Design

Compared static, rule-based, interpretable probabilistic, and Random-Forest hurricane-risk representations inside a two-stage stochastic CVaR supply-chain network design model. Built an event-node dataset from 464 North Atlantic hurricanes and validated reduced-scenario designs through full 464-scenario re-evaluation.

`Python` · `scikit-learn` · `Gurobi` · `Stochastic Optimization` · `CVaR` · `Supply Chain Resilience`
